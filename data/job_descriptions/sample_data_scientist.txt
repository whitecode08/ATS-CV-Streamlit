Senior Data Scientist — AI/ML Platform Team

About the Role
We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our AI/ML Platform team.
You will design, build, and deploy machine learning models and data pipelines
that power our core product features. You'll collaborate closely with product,
engineering, and analytics teams to translate business problems into
data-driven solutions.

Key Responsibilities
- Design and implement end-to-end machine learning pipelines (data ingestion,
  feature engineering, model training, evaluation, deployment)
- Build and maintain NLP models for text classification, information extraction,
  and semantic search
- Develop and optimize recommendation systems and ranking algorithms
- Collaborate with MLOps engineers to deploy models to production using Docker
  and Kubernetes
- Write clean, well-documented Python code following software engineering best practices
- Mentor junior data scientists and conduct code reviews
- Communicate complex findings and model results to non-technical stakeholders

Required Skills & Qualifications
- 5+ years of professional experience in data science or machine learning
- Proficiency in Python (pandas, numpy, scikit-learn, PyTorch or TensorFlow)
- Deep expertise in NLP: transformers, BERT, spaCy, tokenization, embeddings
- Experience with LLM fine-tuning, RAG architectures, and prompt engineering
- Solid SQL and NoSQL database experience (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, BigQuery)
- Experience with cloud platforms: AWS (SageMaker, S3, Lambda) or GCP (Vertex AI)
- Familiarity with MLOps tools: MLflow, DVC, Airflow, Docker, Kubernetes
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field

Nice to Have
- Experience with distributed computing: Apache Spark, Dask
- Knowledge of A/B testing and causal inference
- FastAPI or Flask for model serving
- Contributions to open-source ML projects
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, Plotly)

What We Offer
- Competitive salary and equity package
- Remote-first culture with flexible working hours
- Annual learning budget for conferences, courses, and certifications
- Collaborative, diverse, and inclusive team environment
